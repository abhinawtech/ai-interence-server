# Company Remote Work Policy

## Overview
Our company supports flexible work arrangements to promote work-life balance and productivity.

## Remote Work Guidelines
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Must maintain core collaboration hours (10 AM - 3 PM local time)
- Required to attend weekly team meetings in person
- Home office must have reliable internet (minimum 25 Mbps)

## Equipment and Security
- Company laptop with VPN access required
- Secure file sharing through approved platforms only
- Regular security training compliance mandatory

## Performance Expectations
- Maintain same productivity standards as office work
- Regular check-ins with manager (weekly 1:1s)
- Timely response to communications during core hours
